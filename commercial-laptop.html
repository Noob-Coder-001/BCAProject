Commercial Laptop Requirements

Purpose

A laptop suitable for business and professional use.

Specifications

  • Minimum 8GB RAM
  • Intel Core i5 processor or equivalent
  • 256GB SSD storage or higher
  • Integrated GPU

Battery Life

At least 8 hours of battery life for all-day productivity.

Portability

Thin and lightweight design for easy portability during business travel.

Display

Full HD (1920x1080) resolution for clear presentations and video conferencing.

Keyboard and Trackpad

Comfortable and quiet keyboard with a responsive trackpad for efficient work.

Ports

Include a variety of ports such as USB, HDMI, and Ethernet for business connectivity.

Budget

Set a reasonable budget based on required specifications and features for business needs.

Brand Reputation

Choose reputable brands known for reliability, security, and professional support.
